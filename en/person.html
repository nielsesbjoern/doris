About

Doris Gunsch

  • Graduate Psychologist (PPT)
  • Psychological Management Consultant
  • Systemic Coach

For over 20 years, I have supported people, teams, and organisations as a psychological management consultant in realising their potential, developing action-oriented strategies, and implementing them consistently.

For five years I led a training institute for trainers, organisational consultants, mediators, and coaches — and I remain active today as a coach trainer and master trainer for various institutes.

Consulting expertise, project experience, and industry knowledge from various management consulting firms inform my coaching and consulting work with middle and senior management.

My work draws on current findings from neuroscience and health and personality psychology — deepened through teaching at the University of Osnabrück and long-standing membership of Prof. Julius Kuhl's team. With high-quality diagnostic instruments, I work toward the potential development of leaders and teams.

Professional experience
  • Independent Psychological Management Consultant, since 2007
  • Lecturer at the University of Osnabrück, 2009–2011
  • Trainer at management consulting firms (Scan-Up AG, Impart GmbH, Neun Consulting Group, Emprise AG), 1995–2007
  • Director of a training institute for trainers, coaches, organisational consultants, and mediators, 1998–2003
  • Counselling centre work, 1993–1998
