Services overview

Coaching, training and process facilitation for leaders in mid-sized business, healthcare and public administration — psychologically grounded and applied clearly. Where helpful, I add validated PSI diagnostics. Grounded at the University of Osnabrück.

Personal and business coaching for complex leadership situations — from clarification to a concrete course of action, including smart coaching and coaching-on-the-job.
