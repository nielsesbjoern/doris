Diagnostics and Profiling

You want to understand where energy is lost, why certain situations are especially demanding — or which development steps truly support your leadership role? I use scientifically validated potential analyses for this — explained clearly and directly usable in coaching.

Typical situations

  • Pinpointing energy loss and strain patterns in leaders
  • Reflection coaching with a quasi-objective basis when everyday feedback is missing
  • Matching development measures for individuals, teams or hiring decisions
What PSI means in practice

PSI stands for Personality Systems Interaction theory. It describes how stable and situational personality components interact — not as a label („type A“), but functionally: what happens under pressure, which strategies help, which cost energy?

  • More precise diagnosis instead of quick labelling
  • Starting points for coaching and team development
  • Less gut feeling, more clarity for important decisions

PSI is scientific personality psychology — not esotericism and not a simple online personality test.

Diagnostics

The focus is on questions of overcoming performance, willpower, or emotional blocks, as well as individual prevention.

  • Differentiated motive diagnostics for athletes and leaders — including measurement of determining unconscious motive dispositions
  • Burnout cause diagnostics for functional therapeutic coaching
  • Root-cause analysis of energy loss and performance blocks for functional block removal
  • Self-Care Profile© as complementary burnout prevention or for optimal preparation in phases of strain and threat
  • Expert assessment enquiries

Profiling

Various clients engage me for personality profiling questions — from criminological plausibility enquiries to role preparation for actors. In business contexts, the following occasions are most common:

  • Person-centred determination of appropriate human resources development measures
  • Development centre — determining which training a person, group, or team will benefit from most
  • Fit for specific areas of responsibility before hiring
  • Predictive leadership profile for optimising leadership competence
  • Predictive sales profile for optimising sales competence
  • Master modelling — identifying success factors of the „best performers"
  • Self-reflection on one's own personality profile

Potential analyses

Potential analyses for self-development are offered to senior leaders by their organisation or sought independently — as a quasi-objective foundation for reflection coaching, when everyday feedback is lacking.

The potential analysis used combines various quality procedures that can also be deployed individually — e.g. as part of a training programme or as a self-regulation profile. They capture more than a hundred personality factors and relate them to one another on a scientifically grounded basis.

Benefits

  • Understanding of your own development and response patterns
  • Awareness of your own success factors and strengths
  • Insights into energy gain or energy loss dynamics
  • Direct starting points for coaching

In pivot point analysis, the factor with the greatest possible effect on reducing stress and tension — but also on unfolding potential — can be identified.

The main procedure describes not only willpower or motivation relative to the age group, but also identifies potential in analytics, assertiveness, and empathy — with unique differentiation of the processes behind observable traits. I use the world's most comprehensive personality diagnostics, which functionally describes the interplay of personality dimensions and excludes stereotyped thinking.

How does this person organise their stable and unstable personality traits under current strain — which strategies for task requirements are above average, which still need to be developed?

Computer-based leadership and sales assessment

Simple, brief, precise, not manipulable. Results do not depend on conscious deliberation — the advantage over questionnaire methods lies in the objective measurement of central self-regulation functions.

Objectively determined in approximately 40 minutes

  • How well intention formation succeeds in implementing goals
  • How strongly implementation capacity is developed
  • How well one can motivate oneself positively for challenges
  • How quickly one recovers from setbacks and releases negative moods

In addition, competence in implementing the basic motives of power, achievement, and affiliation is captured — as motivational energy sources in the areas of assertion, performance readiness, and relationship management.

I will gladly explain further procedures and applications when planning your measures.
