Team and Process Facilitation

Support for team development, team dynamics, and restructuring — jointly identifying needs and potential, with a team analysis if required.

On request, I support you and your team through team development, clarifying team dynamics, or as a supportive resource during restructuring and implementation phases. Resistance often arises at development-sensitive points — together we identify the needs and potential of your team.

My offering

  • Process facilitation through external project leadership Security and continuity in implementation, with ongoing optimisation through course correction.
  • Conflict management and moderation Early prevention of entrenched conflict — in teams, between departments, or through mediation.
  • Team impulse coaching Assessing the current state, defining challenges, and developing interventions.
  • Team development Guidance from one stage of development to the next — especially during team mergers.
