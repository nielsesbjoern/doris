Training and Seminars

Practical, scientifically grounded seminar and training topics for developing your leadership and sales strength, or for the wellbeing of your employees within occupational health management (OHM). I deliver in-house — all formats are aligned with your strategic objectives and individually composed.

Given the seniority of many clients, I prefer small seminar groups — all topics are also available as individual training or small-group training. All modules, including within cascades, can be booked individually.

Leadership training

Leadership qualification „Across Seven Bridges to Leadership"

Training cascade from developing leadership awareness to the methodical implementation of leadership tasks.

  • Leadership perspectives — Attitude, outlook, and responsibilities; developing leadership awareness
  • Leading through goals and with commitment — Leadership paradigm, implementation work, goal work
  • Leadership instruments — Types of conversation, methods, and steering techniques
  • Employee and team leadership — Techniques for leading, directing, and guiding
  • Leadership strategy — Assertion techniques, stratagems, positioning; making implicit leadership knowledge explicit
  • Leading in change processes — Change management, mood management, delicate situations
  • Leadership and work organisation — Time management and self-management strategies
