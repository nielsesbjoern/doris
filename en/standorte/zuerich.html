Coaching for leaders — Zurich

Support for leadership in international, demanding and often cross-border work contexts.

Leadership at Zurich's business and finance hub

Zurich combines high responsibility density, international teams and often short decision paths — with high expectations for clarity and reliability. I support leaders and leadership teams in exactly these settings: when roles, culture and pace need realignment at the same time. My office is in Osnabrück; Zurich mandates often arise through referrals and long-standing collaboration in the German-speaking region.

Typical contexts in Zurich

  • International leadership teams with sites in Switzerland and Germany
  • Role transitions and onboarding at senior leadership level
  • Tensions between performance pressure, confidentiality and team culture
  • Preparing sensitive negotiation and strategy conversations

Typical concerns

  • Leadership questions under high decision pressure and many interfaces
  • Conflicts in performance-oriented teams with different cultures
  • Strained communication in highly visible leadership circles
  • Boundaries and clarity in mandates spanning multiple countries
